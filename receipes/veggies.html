Easy Cabbage Recipe

Easy simple cabbage in 30mins

Cabbage is one of the popular vegetable side dishes that can be eaten with nshima in Zambia. It is often cooked like a stir-fry made with cooking oil, tomatoes, green peppers, and onions. This easy and delicious recipe is a perfect accompaniment for any protein that goes with nshima. Here is a popular Zambian cabbage recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 or ½ head of cabbage, chopped (depending on how big it is)
  • 1-2 medium-sized tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4-5 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they are translucent and fragrant.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes and green pepper to the pan and stir well.
  3. Let them cook for a few minutes until they are softened. Add the chopped cabbage to the pan and stir well to combine with the tomatoes and onions and peppers.
  4. Season with salt. Cover the pan and let the cabbage cook for 5-10 minutes until it is wilted and tender.
  5. Remove the lid and stir the cabbage stir fry again to ensure it is well mixed and cooked through.
  6. Serve as a side dish with nshima or rice and any protein dish such as beef stew.
